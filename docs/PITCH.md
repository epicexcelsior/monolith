## 2. The Problem (30 seconds)

**DeFi is boring.** Here's what happens when someone stakes $1,000 in a vault today:

1. They deposit into a protocol
2. They see a number: *"$1,000.04"*
3. They check back in a week: *"$1,001.12"*
4. They forget about it
5. When yields drop, they withdraw and never come back

**There's no emotion. No social layer. No reason to return.** DeFi protocols have the worst retention in all of tech — most users churn within 30 days.

Meanwhile, people check Snapchat 30x/day for a streak counter. They play Wordle every morning for a colored grid. They spend hours in Clash of Clans managing a village.

**The insight:** People don't come back for yield. They come back for *feeling something* — ownership, competition, social obligation, self-expression.

---

## 3. The Solution (60 seconds)

**The Monolith** is a massively multiplayer game built on Solana where staking USDC isn't a spreadsheet — it's a **living 3D tower**.

**Here's how it works:**
1. You stake USDC to **claim a glowing block** on a shared 3D obelisk
2. Your block starts **Blazing** — bright, visible, beautiful
3. Over time, it **decays** — fading from gold to dim to dark
4. You **tap daily** to recharge it (30 seconds, free)
5. Consecutive daily taps build **streaks** that multiply your charge power
6. You **customize** your block — colors, emoji, name — it represents YOU
7. **Other players** can see your block, poke you to send free energy, and compete with you for leaderboard positions

**The result:** DeFi that feels like a game. A daily ritual. A place you own on a shared monument. Something you actually *want* to check on.

## 4. Why It's Fun (The Core Loop)

Every session is **30 seconds or less**:

```
SCAN  →  See the tower, find your block
ACT   →  Tap to charge, claim, or customize
FEEL  →  Watch the glow burst, haptic feedback, rank change
DONE  →  Close the app, come back tomorrow
```

### The retention hooks:

| Hook | Mechanic | Psychology |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Daily ritual** | Tap to Charge (free, 30 sec) | Habit formation (like brushing teeth) |
| **Streak multiplier** | Day 7 = 2x charge, Day 30 = 3x | Loss aversion ("don't break the streak!") |
| **Visual decay** | Block literally fades if you stop | Ownership anxiety (mild, friendly) |
| **Poke friends** | Send free energy + notification | Social obligation + reciprocity |
| **Leaderboards** | Skyline rank, brightest block, longest streak | Competition + status |
| **Customization** | Color, emoji, name on your block | Self-expression + identity |

### What makes it DIFFERENT from every other DeFi app:
- **Your position is VISIBLE** — not a number in a table, a glowing block on a tower
- **It DECAYS** — you have a reason to come back every single day
- **Other people SEE you** — social proof, competition, neighborhoods
- **It's FAST** — 30 seconds, not 30 minutes of dashboard analysis
- **It's BEAUTIFUL** — 3D tower with particle effects, not a spreadsheet

## 7. Technical Credibility (For Judges / Engineers)

| Layer | Stack | Status |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **On-chain** | Anchor 0.31 / Rust / Solana Devnet | ✅ Deployed, tested |
| **Mobile** | Expo 54 / React 19 / React Three Fiber | ✅ Running on Seeker |
| **3D** | Three.js + InstancedMesh (1000+ blocks, 30+ FPS) | ✅ Optimized |
| **Wallet** | Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA) + Seed Vault | ✅ Integrated |
| **State** | Zustand + local persistence | ✅ Working |
| **Backend** | Supabase + Colyseus (planned) | 🟡 In progress |
| **Design** | Custom Solarpunk design system (8 components) | ✅ Polished |

### Architecture decision highlights:
- **USDC on-chain, game state off-chain** — trust + flexibility (industry standard for web3 games)
- **InstancedMesh** — 1000 blocks in 1 draw call = 60 FPS (vs 5 FPS with individual meshes)
- **Manual Borsh decoding** — React Native Buffer polyfill lacks `readUIntLE()`, solved with DataView
- **Procedural skybox** — GLSL shader, no texture loading, instant render

## 10. Talking Points for Common Questions

**"How is this different from just staking on Drift?"**
> Drift gives you yield. We give you yield + a daily game + social interactions + visual ownership. Same money, 10x the engagement. We'll likely route through Drift for the actual yield.

**"Why would someone come back every day?"**
> Same reason people maintain Snapchat streaks or play Wordle: it's 30 seconds, it feels good, and there's mild social pressure. Your block fading is visible to everyone. Your streak multiplier is valuable. Your friends can poke you.

**"Isn't this just a Ponzi / unsustainable?"**
> No — the USDC sits in a vault (or earns real yield post-MVP). There's no inflationary token. No "earn to play" mechanics. The game is free to maintain (daily tap costs nothing). The staking is real, the yield is real, the game is the engagement layer on top.

**"What happens if nobody plays?"**
> The tower still exists. Blocks fade gracefully. USDC is always withdrawable. There's no death spiral because there's no token to crash. The worst case is a quiet tower — which is actually aesthetically beautiful (dim blocks, few bright ones standing out).

**"Why Solana? Why Seeker?"**
> Sub-second finality makes staking feel instant. Low fees make $1 stakes viable. MWA + Seed Vault makes the wallet experience seamless on mobile. And Seeker needs killer apps — we want to be one.

**"Can agents/bots game this?"**
> Agents are first-class citizens by design. They have their own API, their own rate limits, and they contribute to the tower's liveliness. A tower with bots AND humans is more interesting than humans alone. The Gravity Tax prevents monopolization.
