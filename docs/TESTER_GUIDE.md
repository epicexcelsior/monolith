# Monolith Tester Guide

> Detailed gameplay guide for testers. For quick-start install instructions, see the root [TESTING.md](../TESTING.md).

## Install

1. Get the APK from the link in [TESTING.md](../TESTING.md) (or scan the QR code)
2. Install on your Android device (allow unknown sources)
3. If upgrading from an older version, uninstall the old one first
4. Open "The Monolith"

## Get Test Tokens

### SOL (for transaction fees)
1. Connect your wallet (Phantom or Seed Vault)
2. Go to **Me** tab > **Get Test Tokens**
3. Tap "Get Test SOL" — 2 SOL will be airdropped

### USDC (for staking)
1. On the same screen, tap "Open USDC Faucet"
2. Copy your wallet address shown on screen
3. Paste it on Circle's faucet site and request test USDC

## How to Play

### Claim a Spark
1. Tap any unclaimed block on the tower
2. Choose a color and stake amount
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. A celebration plays and your Spark face appears on the block!

### Charge Daily
- Tap your block > **CHARGE** to restore energy
- Watch your Spark bounce and react when you charge it
- Energy decays over time — charge daily to keep your Spark alive
- Build **streaks** by charging on consecutive days (3d, 7d, 30d = bonus XP)
- Your Spark's face reflects its energy: happy when blazing, drowsy when fading, asleep when dead

### Spark Evolution
- Every charge contributes to permanent evolution progress
- **5 tiers:** Spark > Ember > Flame > Blaze > Beacon
- Higher tiers glow brighter and get more detailed face features (blush, eyebrows, halos)
- Evolution never regresses — even if energy drops, your tier stays
- Check the progress bar in the block inspector to see how close you are to the next tier

### Customize Your Spark
- All 16 colors, 48 emojis, 11 styles, and 7 textures are unlocked for testing
- During onboarding, you pick your Spark's initial personality (face style)
- Styles like Holographic, Neon, Fire, and Aurora overlay on top of the face

### Earn XP
- **Claim**: 100 XP (first block: +200 bonus)
- **Charge**: 25 XP (variable energy: 15-35 per charge, quality brackets)
- **Combo**: Act within 30s for up to 3x multiplier
- **Streaks**: Milestone bonuses at 3, 7, 14, 30 days

### Compete
- Check the **Board** tab for leaderboards (Skyline, Brightest, Streaks, XP)
- Tap any leaderboard entry to fly to that block on the tower
- **Poke** other players' blocks (30s cooldown, they get a push notification)
- Reclaim dormant blocks (0 energy for 3+ days)

## Known Limitations

- Devnet only (no real money)
- Activity feed on Board tab may use generated data when server has no events
- Wallet connection can occasionally be flaky (devnet RPC)
