# Monolith Tester Guide

## Install

1. Get the APK from the latest EAS preview build
2. Install on your Android device (allow unknown sources)
3. Open "The Monolith"

## Get Test Tokens

### SOL (for transaction fees)
1. Connect your wallet (Phantom or Seed Vault)
2. Go to **Me** tab > **Get Test Tokens**
3. Tap "Get Test SOL" — 2 SOL will be airdropped

### USDC (for staking)
1. On the same screen, tap "Open USDC Faucet"
2. Copy your wallet address shown on screen
3. Paste it on Circle's faucet site and request test USDC

## How to Play

### Claim a Block
1. Tap any unclaimed block on the tower (marked "OPEN")
2. Choose a color and stake amount
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Your block lights up on the tower!

### Charge Daily
- Tap your block > **CHARGE** to restore energy
- Energy decays over time — charge daily to keep your block alive
- Build **streaks** by charging on consecutive days (3d, 7d, 30d = bonus XP)

### Earn XP
- **Claim**: 100 XP (first block: +200 bonus)
- **Charge**: 25 XP + streak milestone bonuses
- **Customize**: 10 XP
- **Combo**: Act within 30s for up to 3x multiplier

### Compete
- Check the **Board** tab for leaderboards (Skyline, Brightest, Streaks, Territory)
- Tap any leaderboard entry to fly to that block
- Reclaim dormant blocks (0 energy for 3+ days)

## Known Limitations

- Devnet only (no real money)
- Sound effects not yet wired
- Push notifications not yet implemented
- Activity feed on Board tab uses generated data (real-time ticker works on tower view)
